Terms & Conditions

1. EMI Availability

EMI facility for current bill is available only at Customer Care Centers (CCC).
In exceptional high-risk cases, Online EMI may be allowed with special approval.

2. EMI Eligibility Conditions

Only one EMI facility is allowed per consumer per financial year.
For additional EMI, consumer must visit the nearest CCC.

3. EMI Not Applicable For:

  • Consumers with Live connections
  • TD less than 6 months
  • EMI on current month bill (unless via CCC)
  • Consumers with previous check bounce records
  • Vigilance or Theft Investigation cases

Note:

EMI approval is subject to internal risk assessment.
Ineligible or fraudulent cases may lead to cancellation without prior notice.
